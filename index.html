Sushi Go

Create the most delicious sushi combinations in this fast-paced card drafting game!

How to Play Sushi Go

Sushi Go is a fast-paced card drafting game where players try to create the most delicious sushi combinations. Each round, players select cards from their hand to play, then pass the remaining cards to their neighbor. Score points by collecting sets of sushi, creating maki rolls, or gathering wasabi to enhance your nigiri!

Game Features

  • Quick and easy to learn
  • Perfect for 2-5 players
  • Strategic card drafting gameplay
  • Beautiful sushi-themed artwork
  • Multiple scoring opportunities

Tips for Success

Keep an eye on what your opponents are collecting and try to anticipate their moves. Sometimes it's better to deny your opponent a valuable card than to take a slightly better one for yourself. Remember that wasabi can triple the value of your nigiri, so plan your combinations carefully!
